Tetris is a video game conceived and programmed by the Russian computer engineer Alexei Pezhitnov, while working at the Computing Center of the Soviet Academy of Sciences. The name of the game is derived from combining the number four in Greek, "Tetra" together with the name of Pezhitnov's favorite sport, tennis.
